Import of E-Waste Permissions

Import of e-waste is strictly regulated to prevent environmental and health hazards. Special permissions are required from government authorities.

Regulatory Authority

  • Ministry of Environment
  • Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB)

Conditions for Import

  • Only for authorized recycling
  • Compliance with environmental rules
  • Proper documentation and approval

Conclusion

Strict control on e-waste import helps protect the environment.
