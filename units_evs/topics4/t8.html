Monitoring of Compliance of Rules

Monitoring ensures that e-waste management rules and environmental regulations are properly followed.

Monitoring Authorities

  • Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB)
  • State Pollution Control Boards (SPCB)

Methods of Monitoring

  • Regular inspections
  • Submission of reports
  • Audits and verification

Purpose

  • Ensure compliance with laws
  • Prevent environmental violations

Conclusion

Effective monitoring helps maintain legal and environmental standards.
