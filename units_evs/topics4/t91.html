Reduction of Waste at Source

Reduction of waste at source means minimizing the generation of e-waste before it becomes waste.

Methods

  • Designing durable electronic products
  • Using eco-friendly materials
  • Encouraging repair instead of replacement
  • Promoting reuse of devices

Benefits

  • Less environmental pollution
  • Conservation of resources
  • Reduced disposal costs

Conclusion

Preventing waste generation is the most effective way to manage e-waste.
