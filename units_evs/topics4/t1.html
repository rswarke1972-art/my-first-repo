Health Safeguards in India

India has implemented health safeguards to protect workers and the public from hazards caused by e-waste.

Main Safeguards

  • Use of protective equipment
  • Proper waste handling procedures
  • Safe recycling practices
  • Health monitoring of workers

Role of Government

  • Establish safety standards
  • Conduct inspections
  • Enforce environmental laws

Conclusion

Health safeguards help reduce risks associated with e-waste handling.
