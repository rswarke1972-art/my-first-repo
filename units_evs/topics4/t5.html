Producer Public Government Cooperation

Effective e-waste management requires cooperation between producers, the public, and the government.

Role of Producers

  • Design eco-friendly products
  • Implement take-back programs
  • Follow EPR guidelines

Role of Public

  • Proper disposal of e-waste
  • Participation in recycling programs

Role of Government

  • Enforce regulations
  • Create awareness
  • Monitor compliance

Conclusion

Joint responsibility ensures effective and sustainable e-waste management.
