Regulatory Mechanism

Regulatory mechanisms are systems established to control and manage e-waste effectively.

Main Components

  • Environmental laws and rules
  • Licensing of recyclers
  • Inspection and monitoring
  • Penalties for violations

Objective

  • Ensure safe disposal of e-waste
  • Protect public health
  • Maintain environmental quality

Conclusion

A strong regulatory mechanism ensures proper control of e-waste management.
