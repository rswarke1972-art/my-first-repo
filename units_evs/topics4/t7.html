Engineering Controls

Engineering controls involve technical measures designed to reduce exposure to hazards during e-waste processing.

Main Engineering Measures

  • Proper ventilation systems
  • Dust collection systems
  • Enclosed recycling units
  • Safe dismantling equipment

Purpose

  • Minimize worker exposure
  • Reduce environmental contamination

Conclusion

Engineering controls provide physical protection against e-waste related hazards.
