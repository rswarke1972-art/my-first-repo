Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR)

Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) is a policy approach in which producers are responsible for the collection, recycling, and disposal of their products after end of life.

Main Objectives

  • Reduce environmental impact of products
  • Promote recycling and reuse
  • Encourage eco-friendly product design

Benefits

  • Shared responsibility
  • Improved waste management
  • Resource conservation

Conclusion

EPR ensures that producers take accountability for e-waste management.
