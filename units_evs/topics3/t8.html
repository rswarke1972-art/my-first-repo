Domestic E-Waste Disposal

Domestic e-waste refers to electronic devices discarded by households, such as mobile phones, computers, and appliances.

Common Domestic E-Waste

  • Mobile phones
  • Televisions
  • Computers and laptops
  • Refrigerators

Proper Disposal Methods

  • Give to authorized recycling centers
  • Use government collection programs
  • Donate reusable devices

Conclusion

Responsible disposal of domestic e-waste reduces environmental and health risks.
