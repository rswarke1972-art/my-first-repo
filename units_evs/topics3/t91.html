Technologies for Resource Recovery

Resource recovery technologies are used to extract valuable materials from e-waste.

Main Technologies

  • Mechanical separation
  • Hydrometallurgical processes
  • Pyrometallurgical processes
  • Bioleaching

Recovered Materials

  • Gold
  • Copper
  • Silver
  • Aluminium

Conclusion

Advanced technologies help recover valuable resources from e-waste safely.
