Global Context of E-Waste

E-Waste has become a global environmental issue due to rapid digitalization and technological development worldwide.

Global Scenario

  • Millions of tons of e-waste generated annually
  • Developed countries produce high volumes
  • Developing countries often receive e-waste

Challenges

  • Improper recycling practices
  • Illegal dumping
  • Lack of awareness

International Efforts

  • Basel Convention
  • Global recycling initiatives

Conclusion

E-waste management requires international cooperation and regulation.
