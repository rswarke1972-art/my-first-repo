Effects of E-Waste on Human Health

Exposure to hazardous substances in e-waste can cause serious health problems, especially for workers involved in recycling.

Health Effects

  • Respiratory problems
  • Skin irritation
  • Nervous system damage
  • Kidney and liver disorders
  • Cancer risks

High Risk Groups

  • Informal recycling workers
  • Children living near dumping sites

Conclusion

Proper management of e-waste is essential to protect human health.
