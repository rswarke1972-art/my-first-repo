Environmental Health Aspects

Improper management of e-waste can negatively affect environmental health and overall ecosystem balance.

Environmental Risks

  • Soil contamination
  • Groundwater pollution
  • Air pollution from burning waste
  • Damage to biodiversity

Preventive Measures

  • Safe recycling practices
  • Strict environmental regulations
  • Public awareness programs

Conclusion

Proper e-waste management protects both environmental and public health.
