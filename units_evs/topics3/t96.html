Occupational Health Aspects

Workers involved in e-waste recycling are exposed to hazardous substances, which may affect their health.

Health Risks

  • Exposure to toxic metals
  • Respiratory problems
  • Skin disorders
  • Neurological damage

Safety Measures

  • Use of protective equipment
  • Proper ventilation
  • Training and awareness

Conclusion

Ensuring occupational safety is essential in e-waste management.
