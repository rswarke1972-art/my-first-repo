Hazardous Properties of E-Waste

E-Waste possesses hazardous properties due to the presence of toxic metals and chemical substances.

Main Hazardous Properties

  • Toxicity
  • Flammability
  • Corrosiveness
  • Reactivity

Health Risks

  • Damage to nervous system
  • Respiratory disorders
  • Kidney and liver damage

Environmental Risks

  • Soil degradation
  • Water contamination

Conclusion

Improper handling of e-waste poses serious health and environmental hazards.
