Composition of E-Waste

E-Waste (Electronic Waste) consists of discarded electrical and electronic devices such as computers, mobile phones, televisions, and household appliances.

Main Components

  • Metals (Copper, Aluminium, Gold, Silver)
  • Plastics
  • Glass
  • Circuit boards

Hazardous Substances

  • Lead
  • Mercury
  • Cadmium
  • Brominated flame retardants

Conclusion

E-waste contains both valuable materials and hazardous substances, making proper management essential.
