Resource Recovery Potential of E-Waste

E-Waste contains valuable materials that can be recovered and reused, reducing the need for mining natural resources.

Valuable Materials

  • Precious metals (Gold, Silver, Platinum)
  • Base metals (Copper, Aluminium)
  • Plastics

Benefits

  • Conservation of natural resources
  • Reduction in environmental damage
  • Economic benefits from recovered materials

Conclusion

Proper recycling can transform e-waste into a valuable resource.
