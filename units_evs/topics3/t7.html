Effects of E-Waste on Environment

Improper disposal of e-waste causes serious environmental damage due to toxic substances.

Environmental Impacts

  • Soil contamination
  • Water pollution
  • Air pollution from burning components
  • Loss of biodiversity

Long-Term Effects

  • Bioaccumulation of toxic metals
  • Damage to ecosystems

Conclusion

Safe recycling and disposal are necessary to reduce environmental harm.
