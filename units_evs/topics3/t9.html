Principles of E-Waste Management

E-Waste management focuses on reducing environmental and health risks through proper handling and recycling.

Main Principles

  • Reduce – Minimize electronic waste generation
  • Reuse – Extend life of electronic products
  • Recycle – Recover valuable materials
  • Recover – Extract useful resources

Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR)

  • Manufacturers responsible for product disposal

Conclusion

Effective e-waste management requires responsibility from producers and consumers.
