Steps in Recycling and Recovery

Recycling and recovery of e-waste involves systematic steps to safely extract useful materials.

Main Steps

  • Collection of e-waste
  • Segregation and sorting
  • Dismantling of components
  • Shredding and mechanical separation
  • Material recovery processes

Objective

  • Reduce environmental harm
  • Recover valuable resources

Conclusion

Proper recycling ensures safe and efficient recovery of materials.
