Technologies for Material Recovery

Material recovery technologies are used to extract valuable metals and components from processed e-waste.

Main Technologies

  • Hydrometallurgy (chemical extraction)
  • Pyrometallurgy (high-temperature extraction)
  • Electrometallurgy
  • Bioleaching

Recovered Materials

  • Gold
  • Silver
  • Copper
  • Rare earth elements

Conclusion

Advanced recovery technologies help utilize valuable resources from electronic waste.
