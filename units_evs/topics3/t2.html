Generation of E-Waste

E-Waste is generated due to rapid technological advancement and increased use of electronic devices.

Main Reasons

  • Frequent product upgrades
  • Short life span of gadgets
  • Consumer demand for new technology

Major Sources

  • Households
  • Offices and industries
  • Educational institutions

Conclusion

Improper disposal of increasing e-waste creates serious environmental problems.
