E-Waste Pollutants

E-Waste contains several toxic substances that can cause environmental and health hazards.

Major Pollutants

  • Lead
  • Mercury
  • Cadmium
  • Chromium
  • Brominated flame retardants

Sources

  • Printed circuit boards
  • Batteries
  • Cathode ray tubes (CRT)

Impact

  • Soil contamination
  • Water pollution
  • Air pollution

Conclusion

Toxic pollutants in e-waste require safe handling and disposal.
