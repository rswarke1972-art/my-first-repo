Water Pollution

Water Pollution occurs when harmful substances enter water bodies such as rivers, lakes, and oceans, making the water unsafe.

Causes

  • Industrial waste discharge
  • Sewage disposal
  • Agricultural runoff
  • Oil spills

Impact

  • Harms aquatic life
  • Contaminates drinking water
  • Spreads diseases

Conclusion

Water pollution threatens both human and aquatic life.
