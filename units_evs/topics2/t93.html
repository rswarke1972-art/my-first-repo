Sources of Noise Pollution

Noise pollution is mainly caused by human activities in urban areas.

Main Sources

  • Road traffic and horns
  • Railways and airports
  • Industrial machines
  • Construction work
  • Loudspeakers and music systems

Natural Sources

  • Thunderstorms
  • Volcanic eruptions

Conclusion

Urbanization and industrialization are major contributors to noise pollution.
