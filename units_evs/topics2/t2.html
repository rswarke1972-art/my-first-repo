Types of Pollution

Pollution is classified into different types based on the affected component of environment.

Main Types

  • Air Pollution
  • Water Pollution
  • Noise Pollution
  • Soil Pollution
  • Thermal Pollution

Cause

Most types of pollution are caused by industrialization, urbanization and human activities.

Conclusion

Different types of pollution affect the environment in different ways.
