Effects of Solid Waste Pollution

Improper management of solid waste causes serious environmental and health problems.

Environmental Effects

  • Soil contamination
  • Water pollution due to leachate
  • Air pollution from burning waste
  • Harm to wildlife

Health Effects

  • Spread of diseases
  • Respiratory problems
  • Skin infections

Conclusion

Proper waste disposal and recycling are necessary to reduce solid waste pollution.
