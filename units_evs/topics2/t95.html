Solid Waste Pollution

Solid Waste Pollution occurs due to improper disposal of solid materials such as garbage, plastic, and industrial waste.

Types of Solid Waste

  • Domestic waste
  • Industrial waste
  • Biomedical waste
  • Plastic waste

Problems Caused

  • Land degradation
  • Soil contamination
  • Spread of diseases

Conclusion

Proper waste management is essential to control solid waste pollution.
