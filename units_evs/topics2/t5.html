Effects of Air Pollution

Air pollution has harmful effects on human health, animals, plants, and the environment.

Effects on Human Health

  • Respiratory diseases
  • Asthma and bronchitis
  • Eye and skin irritation
  • Heart problems

Effects on Environment

  • Global warming
  • Acid rain
  • Damage to crops and forests

Conclusion

Air pollution threatens life and must be controlled for a healthy environment.
