Effects of Water Pollution

Water pollution has serious effects on human health, aquatic life, and the environment.

Effects on Human Health

  • Water-borne diseases
  • Cholera and typhoid
  • Skin infections

Effects on Aquatic Life

  • Death of fish and marine animals
  • Disruption of aquatic ecosystem

Environmental Effects

  • Eutrophication
  • Loss of biodiversity

Conclusion

Water pollution damages ecosystems and threatens public health.
