Definition of Pollution

Pollution is the introduction of harmful substances into the environment, causing adverse effects on living organisms.

Definition

  • Undesirable change in physical, chemical or biological characteristics
  • Caused mainly by human activities

Pollutants

  • Solid pollutants
  • Liquid pollutants
  • Gaseous pollutants

Conclusion

Pollution disturbs the natural balance of the environment.
