Types of Environment

Environment can be classified into different types based on its nature and components.

1. Natural Environment

  • Includes land, water, air, plants and animals
  • Exists naturally without human intervention

2. Man-Made (Artificial) Environment

  • Created by humans
  • Includes buildings, roads, industries

3. Social Environment

  • Includes society, culture, traditions and values

Conclusion

Different types of environment influence human life and development.
