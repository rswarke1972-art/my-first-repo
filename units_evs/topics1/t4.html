Man Environment Relationship

Humans and the environment are closely connected. Human survival depends on natural resources provided by the environment.

Dependence on Environment

  • Food
  • Water
  • Air
  • Shelter

Impact of Human Activities

  • Industrialization
  • Urbanization
  • Deforestation
  • Pollution

Balanced Relationship

Sustainable use of resources is necessary to maintain balance.

Conclusion

Humans must protect the environment to ensure long-term survival.
