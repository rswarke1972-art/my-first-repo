Sustainable Environment

A Sustainable Environment refers to the responsible use of natural resources to meet present needs without compromising future generations.

Concept of Sustainability

  • Conservation of natural resources
  • Reduction of pollution
  • Efficient use of energy

Methods to Achieve Sustainability

  • Use of renewable energy
  • Recycling and waste management
  • Afforestation
  • Water conservation

Conclusion

Sustainable practices help protect the environment for future generations.
