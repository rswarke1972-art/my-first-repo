Need for Public Awareness

Public awareness is essential to protect and conserve the environment. People must understand environmental problems and their solutions.

Reasons for Awareness

  • Control pollution
  • Conserve natural resources
  • Promote sustainable development
  • Protect biodiversity

Methods of Creating Awareness

  • Education
  • Media campaigns
  • Government policies
  • Community programs

Conclusion

Public participation is crucial for environmental protection.
