Importance of Environment

The environment plays a vital role in supporting life on Earth. It provides essential resources and maintains ecological balance.

Importance

  • Provides food and water
  • Supplies oxygen for breathing
  • Regulates climate
  • Supports biodiversity

Economic Importance

  • Natural resources for industries
  • Agricultural production

Conclusion

Protecting the environment is necessary for present and future generations.
