Major Components of Ecosystem

An ecosystem consists of two major components: biotic and abiotic components.

1. Biotic Components

  • Producers (Plants)
  • Consumers (Animals)
  • Decomposers (Bacteria and Fungi)

2. Abiotic Components

  • Air
  • Water
  • Soil
  • Sunlight
  • Temperature

Interaction

Energy flows from producers to consumers and nutrients are recycled by decomposers.

Conclusion

Both components work together to maintain ecological balance.
