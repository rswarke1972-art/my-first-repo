Components of Environment

The environment consists of different components that interact with each other.

1. Biotic Components

  • All living organisms
  • Plants
  • Animals
  • Microorganisms

2. Abiotic Components

  • Non-living elements
  • Air
  • Water
  • Soil
  • Sunlight

Interaction

Biotic and abiotic components depend on each other for survival.

Conclusion

Both living and non-living components together form the environment.
