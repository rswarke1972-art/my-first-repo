Hasse Diagram

A Hasse Diagram is a graphical representation of a partially ordered set (poset). It shows the ordering of elements without drawing all transitive edges.

Characteristics

  • Used for partial order relations
  • No loops are shown
  • Transitive edges are omitted
  • Higher elements are drawn above lower elements

Example

For set A = {1, 2, 4} with divisibility relation:

  • 1 divides 2
  • 2 divides 4

Hasse Diagram shows: 1 at bottom → 2 above → 4 at top

Conclusion

Hasse diagrams provide a simplified visual representation of partial order relations.
