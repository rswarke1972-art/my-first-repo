Growth of Functions

Growth of functions describes how a function behaves as the input size increases. It is important in analyzing algorithms.

Common Growth Rates

  • Constant – O(1)
  • Logarithmic – O(log n)
  • Linear – O(n)
  • Quadratic – O(n²)
  • Exponential – O(2ⁿ)

Big-O Notation

  • Describes upper bound of growth
  • Focuses on worst-case scenario

Importance

  • Compares algorithm efficiency
  • Helps in optimization

Conclusion

Growth of functions helps evaluate performance of algorithms as input increases.
