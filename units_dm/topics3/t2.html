Properties of Trees

Trees have several important mathematical properties that distinguish them from other types of graphs.

Main Properties

  • A tree with n vertices has exactly n - 1 edges
  • There is exactly one simple path between any two vertices
  • A tree is connected and acyclic
  • Adding one edge creates a cycle
  • Removing one edge disconnects the tree

Leaf Nodes

  • A tree with at least two vertices has at least two leaf nodes

Conclusion

These properties help identify and analyze tree structures in graph theory.
