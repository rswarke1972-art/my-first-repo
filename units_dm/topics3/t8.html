Max Flow: Min Cut Theorem

The Max Flow - Min Cut Theorem states that the maximum flow from source to sink in a network equals the capacity of the minimum cut.

Flow Network

  • Directed graph
  • Edges have capacities
  • One source and one sink

Max Flow

  • Maximum possible flow from source to sink

Min Cut

  • Minimum set of edges whose removal separates source and sink

Application

  • Network routing
  • Traffic management

Conclusion

Maximum flow in a network is equal to the capacity of its minimum cut.
