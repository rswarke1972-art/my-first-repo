Pascal's Triangle

Pascal's Triangle is a triangular arrangement of binomial coefficients. Each number is the sum of the two numbers directly above it.

Structure

1 1 1 1 2 1 1 3 3 1 1 4 6 4 1

Properties

  • Row n represents coefficients of (a + b)^n
  • First and last elements of each row are 1
  • Triangle is symmetric

Application

  • Binomial expansion
  • Combinatorics

Conclusion

Pascal's Triangle provides a simple way to compute binomial coefficients.
