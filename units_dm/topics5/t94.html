Groups

A Group is an algebraic structure that consists of a set with a binary operation satisfying certain properties.

Definition

  • Closure: For all a, b ∈ G, a * b ∈ G
  • Associativity: (a * b) * c = a * (b * c)
  • Identity Element: There exists e such that a * e = e * a = a
  • Inverse Element: For every a ∈ G, there exists b such that a * b = e

Example

(Set of integers, +)

  • Identity = 0
  • Inverse of a = -a

Conclusion

A group has closure, associativity, identity, and inverse properties.
