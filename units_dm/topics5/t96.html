Normal Subgroups

A Normal Subgroup is a subgroup that is invariant under conjugation by elements of the group.

Definition

  • A subgroup N of G is normal if
  • gNg⁻¹ = N for all g ∈ G

Notation

N ◁ G

Importance

  • Used to form quotient groups

Example

Every subgroup of an abelian group is normal.

Conclusion

Normal subgroups are essential for constructing new group structures.
