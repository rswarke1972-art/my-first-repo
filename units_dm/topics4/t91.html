Planar Graphs

A Planar Graph is a graph that can be drawn on a plane without any edges crossing.

Definition

  • Edges intersect only at their endpoints

Euler's Formula

For a connected planar graph:

V - E + F = 2

  • V = Number of vertices
  • E = Number of edges
  • F = Number of faces

Non-Planar Graphs

  • K₅ (Complete graph with 5 vertices)
  • K₃,₃ (Complete bipartite graph)

Conclusion

Planar graphs can be drawn without edge crossings on a plane.
