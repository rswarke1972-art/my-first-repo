Special Types of Graphs

Graphs can be classified into different types based on their structure and properties.

Common Types

1. Simple Graph

  • No loops
  • No multiple edges

2. Multigraph

  • Multiple edges allowed between same vertices

3. Complete Graph

  • Every pair of vertices is connected

4. Bipartite Graph

  • Vertices divided into two disjoint sets
  • Edges connect vertices from different sets

5. Regular Graph

  • All vertices have same degree

Conclusion

Different types of graphs help model various real-world problems.
