Handshaking Lemma

The Handshaking Lemma states that in any undirected graph, the sum of degrees of all vertices is equal to twice the number of edges.

Formula

Sum of degrees = 2 × Number of edges

Important Result

  • The number of vertices with odd degree is always even

Example

If a graph has 3 edges, total degree sum = 2 × 3 = 6

Conclusion

The handshaking lemma helps analyze degree properties in graphs.
