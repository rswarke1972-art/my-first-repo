Types of Actuators

Different types of actuators are used to perform specific mechanical actions in IoT systems.

Common Types

  • DC Motor
  • Servo Motor
  • Stepper Motor
  • Relay
  • Solenoid

Applications

  • Robotics
  • Smart home automation
  • Industrial machines

Conclusion

Actuators enable controlled physical movement in IoT devices.
