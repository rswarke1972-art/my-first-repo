Principles of Sensors

Sensors work based on physical or chemical principles to detect changes in the environment.

Basic Working Principle

  • Detect physical quantity (temperature, light, pressure)
  • Convert it into electrical signal

Common Principles

  • Resistive principle
  • Capacitive principle
  • Inductive principle
  • Piezoelectric principle

Conclusion

Sensors operate by converting environmental changes into measurable signals.
