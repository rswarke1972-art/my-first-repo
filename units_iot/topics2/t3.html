Classification of Sensors

Sensors can be classified based on different criteria such as output signal, energy source, and application.

Based on Output

  • Analog Sensors
  • Digital Sensors

Based on Energy Source

  • Active Sensors
  • Passive Sensors

Based on Measured Quantity

  • Temperature Sensors
  • Pressure Sensors
  • Humidity Sensors
  • Motion Sensors

Conclusion

Classification helps in selecting appropriate sensors for IoT applications.
