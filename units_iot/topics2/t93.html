Specifications of Actuators

Actuator specifications describe the technical parameters and limits of actuator performance.

Common Specifications

  • Operating voltage
  • Power rating
  • Speed (RPM)
  • Torque or force rating
  • Operating temperature

Purpose

  • Ensure compatibility with system
  • Provide safe and efficient operation

Conclusion

Specifications help engineers select appropriate actuators for IoT applications.
