Sensors and Transducers

Sensors and transducers are devices used to detect physical changes and convert them into electrical signals.

Sensor

  • Detects physical parameters
  • Examples: Temperature, pressure, light

Transducer

  • Converts one form of energy into another
  • Example: Microphone converts sound to electrical signal

Conclusion

Sensors and transducers form the basic input devices in IoT systems.
