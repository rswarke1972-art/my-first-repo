Classification of Actuators

Actuators can be classified based on their source of energy and type of motion.

Based on Energy Source

  • Electrical Actuators
  • Hydraulic Actuators
  • Pneumatic Actuators

Based on Motion

  • Linear Actuators
  • Rotary Actuators

Conclusion

Classification helps in selecting appropriate actuators for IoT systems.
