Principles of Actuators

Actuators work by converting electrical energy into mechanical motion or physical action.

Basic Working Principle

  • Receive input signal from controller
  • Convert electrical energy into motion

Common Operating Principles

  • Electromagnetic principle
  • Hydraulic principle
  • Pneumatic principle
  • Thermal principle

Conclusion

Actuators translate control signals into real-world physical movement.
