IEEE 802.11ah

IEEE 802.11ah is a Wi-Fi standard designed for IoT applications, also known as Wi-Fi HaLow.

Features

  • Operates in sub-1 GHz band
  • Long-range communication
  • Low power consumption

Advantages

  • Supports large number of devices
  • Better coverage than traditional Wi-Fi

Conclusion

IEEE 802.11ah enables long-range and low-power IoT connectivity.
