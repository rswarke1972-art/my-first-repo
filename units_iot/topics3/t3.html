IEEE 802.15.4

IEEE 802.15.4 is a standard for low-rate wireless personal area networks (LR-WPAN), widely used in IoT applications.

Features

  • Low power consumption
  • Short-range communication
  • Low data rate

Applications

  • Zigbee networks
  • Wireless sensor networks

Conclusion

IEEE 802.15.4 provides energy-efficient communication for IoT devices.
