BLE (Bluetooth Low Energy)

Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) is a power-efficient version of Bluetooth designed for IoT and battery-operated devices.

Features

  • Very low power consumption
  • Short-range communication
  • Fast connection setup

Applications

  • Fitness trackers
  • Smartwatches
  • Healthcare devices

Conclusion

BLE is ideal for low-power IoT applications.
