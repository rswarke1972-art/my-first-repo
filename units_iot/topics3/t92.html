Zigbee

Zigbee is a low-power, short-range wireless communication technology based on IEEE 802.15.4 standard.

Features

  • Low energy consumption
  • Mesh networking support
  • Low data rate

Applications

  • Home automation
  • Smart lighting systems
  • Wireless sensor networks

Conclusion

Zigbee is suitable for low-power IoT networks.
