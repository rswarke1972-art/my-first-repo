6LoWPAN

6LoWPAN (IPv6 over Low Power Wireless Personal Area Networks) is a protocol that enables IPv6 communication over low-power wireless networks.

Features

  • Supports IPv6
  • Low power consumption
  • Efficient packet transmission

Usage

  • Wireless sensor networks
  • Smart home systems

Conclusion

6LoWPAN allows low-power IoT devices to connect directly to the internet using IPv6.
