LoRaWAN

LoRaWAN (Long Range Wide Area Network) is a low-power wide-area networking protocol designed for IoT applications.

Features

  • Long-range communication
  • Low power consumption
  • Supports large number of devices

Applications

  • Smart cities
  • Agriculture monitoring
  • Industrial IoT

Conclusion

LoRaWAN is ideal for long-distance IoT communication with minimal power usage.
