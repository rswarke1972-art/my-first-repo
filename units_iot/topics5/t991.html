Wearable Electronics

Wearable Electronics are smart devices that can be worn on the body and connected to the internet.

Examples

  • Smartwatches
  • Fitness bands
  • Smart glasses
  • Health monitoring devices

Features

  • Wireless connectivity
  • Real-time data tracking
  • Compact and portable design

Conclusion

Wearable electronics enhance personal monitoring and connectivity using IoT technology.
