Prognostics

Prognostics in IoT refers to predicting the future condition or remaining useful life of systems and equipment.

How It Works

  • Sensors collect performance data
  • Data is analyzed using algorithms
  • System predicts possible failures

Benefits

  • Preventive maintenance
  • Reduced downtime
  • Cost savings

Conclusion

Prognostics helps in early detection and prevention of system failures.
