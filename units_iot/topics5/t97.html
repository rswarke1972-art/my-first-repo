Machine Diagnostics

Machine Diagnostics uses IoT to monitor machine performance and detect faults in industrial systems.

How It Works

  • Sensors collect vibration and temperature data
  • Data is analyzed for abnormalities
  • Alerts are generated for maintenance

Benefits

  • Predictive maintenance
  • Reduced downtime
  • Improved machine efficiency

Conclusion

IoT-based diagnostics improves industrial productivity and safety.
