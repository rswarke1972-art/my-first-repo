River Flood Detection

River Flood Detection systems use IoT to monitor water levels in rivers and provide early flood warnings.

How It Works

  • Water level sensors are installed near rivers
  • Real-time data is sent to monitoring centers
  • Alerts are issued if water crosses safe limits

Benefits

  • Early warning system
  • Reduced property damage
  • Improved disaster management

Conclusion

IoT-based flood detection helps protect lives and property.
