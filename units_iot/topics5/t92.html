Noise Pollution Monitoring

Noise Pollution Monitoring systems use IoT to measure sound levels in different environments.

How It Works

  • Sound sensors detect noise levels
  • Data is transmitted to cloud
  • Authorities monitor and take action

Benefits

  • Control of excessive noise
  • Improved public health
  • Better urban planning

Conclusion

IoT helps manage and reduce noise pollution effectively.
