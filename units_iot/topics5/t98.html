Indoor Air Quality Monitoring

Indoor Air Quality Monitoring systems use IoT to measure air conditions inside buildings and maintain a healthy environment.

Measured Parameters

  • Carbon dioxide (CO₂)
  • Temperature
  • Humidity
  • Particulate matter

Benefits

  • Improved health and comfort
  • Better ventilation control
  • Energy efficiency

Conclusion

IoT-based air monitoring ensures safe and comfortable indoor environments.
