Air Pollution Monitoring

Air Pollution Monitoring systems use IoT to measure and analyze air quality in real time.

Measured Parameters

  • Carbon monoxide (CO)
  • Carbon dioxide (CO₂)
  • Particulate matter (PM2.5, PM10)
  • Other harmful gases

Benefits

  • Improved public health awareness
  • Pollution control measures
  • Environmental monitoring

Conclusion

IoT-based air monitoring helps in maintaining clean and safe environments.
