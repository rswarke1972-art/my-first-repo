Structural Health Monitoring

Structural Health Monitoring (SHM) uses IoT to monitor the condition of structures like bridges and buildings.

How It Works

  • Sensors measure vibration and stress
  • Data is transmitted to cloud
  • System analyzes structural stability

Benefits

  • Early fault detection
  • Improved safety
  • Reduced maintenance cost

Conclusion

SHM ensures safety and longevity of infrastructure using IoT.
