Smart Parking

Smart Parking is an IoT-based system that helps drivers find available parking spaces easily.

How It Works

  • Sensors detect vehicle presence
  • Data is sent to cloud server
  • Users check availability via mobile app

Benefits

  • Reduces traffic congestion
  • Saves fuel and time
  • Improves parking management

Conclusion

Smart parking improves urban transportation efficiency.
