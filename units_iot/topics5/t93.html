Forest Fire Detection

Forest Fire Detection systems use IoT to identify fire hazards at an early stage and prevent large-scale damage.

How It Works

  • Temperature and smoke sensors monitor forest areas
  • Data is sent to control centers
  • Alerts are generated in case of abnormal readings

Benefits

  • Early fire detection
  • Reduced environmental damage
  • Improved disaster response

Conclusion

IoT-based systems help in protecting forests and wildlife from fire hazards.
