Embedded Systems

An Embedded System is a specialized computer system designed to perform a specific task within a larger system.

Components

  • Microcontroller or Microprocessor
  • Memory
  • Input/Output devices

Role in IoT

  • Control sensors and actuators
  • Process data locally
  • Enable device communication

Conclusion

Embedded systems act as the backbone of IoT devices.
