Things in IoT

In IoT, "Things" refer to physical devices that are connected to the internet and capable of collecting and exchanging data.

Examples of Things

  • Sensors
  • Smartphones
  • Wearable devices
  • Smart appliances
  • Industrial machines

Features

  • Unique identification
  • Connectivity
  • Data processing capability

Conclusion

Things are the core components that enable IoT functionality.
