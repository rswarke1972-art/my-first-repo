Evolution of IoT

IoT evolved from the advancement of internet technologies, wireless communication, and embedded systems.

Stages of Evolution

  • Internet connectivity (1990s)
  • Mobile and wireless networks
  • Cloud computing integration
  • Smart devices and sensors

Modern IoT

  • Smart homes
  • Smart cities
  • Industrial IoT (IIoT)

Conclusion

IoT evolved through the integration of communication and computing technologies.
