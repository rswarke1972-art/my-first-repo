Definition of Internet of Things (IoT)

The Internet of Things (IoT) refers to a network of physical devices connected to the internet, which collect and exchange data.

Definition

  • Interconnection of smart devices
  • Devices communicate without human intervention

Examples

  • Smart homes
  • Smart cities
  • Wearable devices

Conclusion

IoT enables automation and intelligent decision-making through connected devices.
