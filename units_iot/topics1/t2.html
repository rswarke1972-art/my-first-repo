Characteristics of IoT

IoT has several unique characteristics that make it different from traditional internet systems.

Main Characteristics

  • Connectivity – Devices are interconnected
  • Intelligence – Smart decision making
  • Automation – Minimal human intervention
  • Scalability – Large number of devices
  • Dynamic nature – Devices join and leave network

Conclusion

These characteristics make IoT a powerful technology for modern systems.
