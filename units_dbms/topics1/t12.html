Database-System Applications

Database systems are widely used in various fields to store, manage, and retrieve data efficiently. Almost every modern application depends on databases.

Major Applications

1. Banking

  • Customer accounts
  • Transactions
  • Loan records

2. Education

  • Student records
  • Exam results
  • Course management

3. E-Commerce

  • Product details
  • Orders and payments
  • Customer information

4. Healthcare

  • Patient records
  • Medical history
  • Appointment scheduling

5. Telecommunications

  • Call records
  • Billing information

Conclusion

Database systems play a vital role in managing data efficiently across various industries and applications.
