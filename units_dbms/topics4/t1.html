Basic Concept of a Transaction

A Transaction is a sequence of database operations that are treated as a single logical unit of work. It must either complete fully or not execute at all.

Examples of Transactions

  • Bank money transfer
  • Online ticket booking
  • Updating student records

Transaction States

  • Active
  • Partially Committed
  • Committed
  • Failed
  • Aborted

Importance

  • Maintains database consistency
  • Prevents data corruption

Conclusion

A transaction ensures reliable and consistent execution of database operations.
