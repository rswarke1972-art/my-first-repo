Concept of Schedule

A Schedule is a sequence of operations from one or more transactions that preserves the order of operations within each individual transaction.

Purpose of Schedule

  • Manage concurrent execution of transactions
  • Ensure consistency of database

Types of Schedules

  • Serial Schedule
  • Non-Serial Schedule

Example

T1: Read(A) → Write(A) T2: Read(B) → Write(B)

Importance

  • Helps analyze concurrency control
  • Prevents conflicts between transactions

Conclusion

A schedule defines the execution order of multiple transactions in a database system.
