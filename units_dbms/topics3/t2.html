Third Normal Form (3NF)

A relation is in Third Normal Form (3NF) if it is in Second Normal Form (2NF) and has no transitive dependency.

Transitive Dependency

  • Occurs when a non-key attribute depends on another non-key attribute

Conditions for 3NF

  • Relation must be in 2NF
  • No transitive dependency

Example

Student (Roll_No, Dept_ID, Dept_Name)

  • Roll_No → Dept_ID
  • Dept_ID → Dept_Name
  • So Roll_No → Dept_Name (Transitive dependency)

Solution

  • Split into separate tables

Conclusion

3NF removes transitive dependencies and further improves database design.
